# Brand Notes

Tone:
- Clear
- Educational
- Calm
- Structured
- Warm and practical

Design Principles:
- Clarity over decoration
- Consistent spacing and hierarchy
- Photography-first when relevant
- Modular components for repeatable layouts
- Warm, natural colour palette
- Accessible typography (Inter family)
- Minimal visual noise

Usage:
These principles apply across cheat sheets, micro manuals, books, wildlife guides, and business OS materials.
